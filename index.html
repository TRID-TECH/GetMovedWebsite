01 — Quote

Start your move request in minutes.

  • Select your origin and destination
  • Choose your preferred moving date
  • Define the type of move (local, long-distance, etc.)
  • Select required services (packing, storage, special items, etc.)
  • Create your inventory manually or with AI assistance
  • Submit your request to all qualified movers in your area

02 — AI

By analyzing uploaded video, AI does the following:

  • Generates a complete, room-by-room inventory list
  • Detects and groups items with accurate quantities
  • Calculates estimated volume and weight
  • Determines the appropriate vehicle type and size
  • Identifies special handling requirements (fragile, bulky, etc.)
  • Creates clear, written instructions for movers

03 — Smart Matching

Once your quote is submitted, GetMoved handles the rest:

  • Identifies all moving companies that meet your criteria (services offered, geographic coverage, availability)
  • Sends a precisely structured move request to qualified movers
  • Enables movers to submit their offers via a secure link within 24 hours
  • Collects and organizes all offers in one clear comparison view
  • Highlights key differences in pricing, services, and timing
  • Allows you to choose a mover independently or with AI assistant recommendations

04 — Seamless Execution

From confirmation to payment — fully managed and automated:

  • Once selected, the mover assigns required vehicles and crew based on structured instructions
  • The move is executed according to the defined inventory and plan
  • Both customer and mover confirm job completion via the mobile app
  • The system automatically generates the final invoice
  • Payment is securely processed from the customer
  • Funds are transferred directly to the moving company

What videos work best?

A slow, steady phone walkthrough with good lighting and open doors. Pan rooms left to right and avoid rapid cuts for best tracking.

How accurate is inventory?

Typical accuracy is 85–95% on common furniture. You can review snapshots and adjust quantities before exporting.

Do you support custom item classes?

Yes. We can train custom classes with labeled examples and add them to your model.

How do you estimate volume/weight?

We combine depth estimation with physics-based priors per item category to generate volume and weight ranges.

Can I export to my TMS/CRM?

Yes. Export JSON/CSV today, and we support API integrations for common systems.

Where does processing run?

Processing runs on our HPC stack in secure cloud regions. Enterprise on-prem options are available.

Commercial launch phase
